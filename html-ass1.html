Chicken Biriyani

Introduction

Biryani is known for its aroma and flavor. Fresh herbs, saffron, and basmati rice give it a sweet-floral aroma that hits the nose before you taste it. Whole garam masala, fried onions, and marinated meat provide warmth and subtle heat to the dish. Overall, it tastes like a savory, spiced rice pilaf.

The rice gets its robust flavor from a variety of spices, including ginger, garlic, nutmeg, cardamom, cinnamon, saffron and rosewater, as well as from the meat itself.

How to prepare the best Chicken Biriyani?

Preparation- Marination:

Marinating chicken with yogurt & spices is the first key step to make the best chicken biriyani. yogurt tenderizes the chicken & the spices enhance the falvors. Marinating and resting chicken makes it soft & succulent, so do not skip that. If you do not eat curd then can substitute it with coconut milk.

Preparing Rice:

Basmati rice ha to be rinsed very well a few times in ample amount of water to get rid of the starch. This prevents the rice grains from turning mushy. Soaking the rice helps to cook the rice grains to fluffy & long. So do not skip rinsing & soaking the rice well before using.

Water:

This recipe uses plain water to cook the chicken biriyani. However it can be replaced with coconut milk or chicken stock. The quantity of water i have mentioned in the recipe card works well for me with Royal Basmati rice. This ratio works pretty well with any gaed basmati rice.

Please do adjust the amount of heat in the recipe. My recipes are usually low on heat for the indian taste buds.
